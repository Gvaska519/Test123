Our Story

Where tradition meets innovation in the heart of Poti

Founded in 2025, Sunset Cafe has quickly established itself as Poti's premier destination for exceptional coffee and refined dining. Our passion for quality ingredients and meticulous preparation sets us apart.

We source our coffee beans from sustainable farms and roast them in-house to ensure the perfect cup every time. Our kitchen combines Georgian culinary traditions with modern techniques to create memorable dishes.

Our cozy yet sophisticated ambiance provides the perfect setting for breakfast meetings, romantic dinners, or simply enjoying a quiet moment with a perfect cup of coffee.
